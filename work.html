Alameda de los Descalzos, Rímac

From march to june 2023, I worked as a Communications Assistant for the environmental consulting company "Forestsoil". During this process, I learned how to effectively apply communication skills to blend and gather crucial information about public opinion on public cultural spaces. Socio-environmental monitoring in Lima

From july 2023 to march 2024, I've been working as Communications Assistant for the environmental consulting company "Forestsoil" and the seed and soil research company "Forestec". During this period, I participated in the field execution and datanalysis process for the socio-environmental monitoring of 4 different government-funded schools, called "Escuelas Bicentenario" in the districts of El Agustino, Breña, Los Olivos and Comas in Lima, Perú. During this process, I learned how to conduct a noise monitoring and analize citizen's opinions on these government funded projects and general education. I worked on SPSS to analize the surveys data and used PowerBI to summarize the report needed to present to the Ministry of Education.

Internal Communication Research

From march to december 2022 I worked as a communications intern at the internal communications area of the government agency "Sunat", doing research of employee's satisfaction and effectiveness. During this time, I learned how to use PowerBI to create a live data base uploaded on the companys cloud that showed, through the use of PowerBI, the current percentage of goal achievement and employee's satisfaction at the current state. This database was refreshed everyday and contained data from all the agencies all across the whole country.
